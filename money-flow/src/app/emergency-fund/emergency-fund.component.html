Emergency Fund

It looks like you have a net negative income. In order to establish an emergency fund, you will have to reassess where spending is necessary in order to net a positive monthly income.

Now that you have some disposable income, make sure to set all ${{netExpenses}} aside into an emergency fund.

After you start to gain some disposable income, make sure to set as much of it as possible, each month, into an emergency fund.

After a couple months, you will have enough to be able to cover a full month of expenses in case of emergency.

Non-Essential Bills

Non-Essential Bills include things like paying for cable, phone, internet, streaming services, and so on.

These are things that are not really necessary but can be accommodated for once enough disposable income is saved.

Moving Forward

Now that you have established your budget and know where to cut back or add funds, you're ready to start increasing your savings in order to pay off debt.
