Welcome to MoneyFlow! Use this interactive tool to get a handle on your financial literacy. You'll initally create a budget in order to evaluate your spending. By assessing your budget, you'll learn to establish and build emergency funds, capitalize on employer-sponsored funding, eliminate debt, and begin to save for the near and distant future.


  • Budgeting - an estimate of expected income and expense for a given period in the future.

  • Emergency Funds - a readily available source of money that is saved specifically to help navigate financial dilemmas such as job loss, a debilitating illness, or a major repair to your home or car.

  • Employer-Sponsored Funds - a type of benefit plan offered to employees which cover an array of services including retirement savings.

  • Debt Reduction - the process of beginning to pay off money that is owed or due.

  • Start Savings - the process of maximizing the money left after all expenses have been paid
